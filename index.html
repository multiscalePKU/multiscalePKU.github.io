Welcome

Currently I am an Assistant Professor in the Department of Mechanics and Engineering Science, of the College of Engineering, Peking University. My current research interests include: AI-inspired multi-scale modeling, Computational multiphysics and Physics of soft and living matter. See the Research section for more details.

Short bio

From 2016-2020, I was a postdoc with Prof. Andrej Košmrlj at Princeton University. In 2016, I obtained my Ph.D. from University of Pennsylvania in 2016 where I was advised by Prof. Prashant Purohit. Before coming to Penn, I obtained my B.S. from Peking University.
